About me

Front-end developer

I'm Willian Matheus, a Computer Science student with a passion for technology, especially front-end development. I'm in my 2nd period at Estácio de Sá, studying EAD, and I have a solid knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, with certifications in these areas. My front-end skills include creating intuitive and responsive user interfaces and applying good design practices to ensure a pleasant and efficient user experience. I have experience using tools like Figma for interface design and prototyping, which allows me to effectively transform concepts into visual reality. My professional journey also includes four years as a Graphic Designer and Customer Service at Our Things Store, as well as experience as an Administration Assistant in the Brazilian Army, where I worked in the IT section. These experiences have given me a solid foundation in design and technical support, which complements my development skills. I'm looking for an opportunity to start my career as a front-end developer, where I can apply my skills, collaborate with a talented team and continue growing professionally.
